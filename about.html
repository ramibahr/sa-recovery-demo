Who We Are

SA Recovery Group was founded with a single purpose: to make sure no driver is ever left stranded on the side of the road. With years of experience in the vehicle recovery industry, our team of certified professionals has helped thousands of motorists across the region.

We operate a modern fleet of recovery vehicles, equipped with the latest tools and technology to handle any situation — from a simple flat tyre to a complex accident recovery.

Our commitment is simple: fast response, professional service, and peace of mind — every single time.
